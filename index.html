Ceradon Mesh Architect v0.3 – RF mesh planner for controllers, relays, sensors, clients, and UxS

Mid-weight RF mesh planning between heavyweight propagation suites and ad-hoc field tests. Tune terrain and EW sliders, plan in the bands you actually fly, and export overlays for TAK.

Demo flow: 1) Pick a preset AO. 2) Drag nodes on the map. 3) Export JSON or ATAK overlay.

Where it fits in the Ceradon Architect Stack

  • Node Architect: define compute + radios + antennas + power.
  • UxS Architect: define air/ground platforms that can carry those nodes.
  • Mesh Architect: place nodes in a real AO and stress RF/EW assumptions.
  • KitSmith / Mission Architect: turn architectures into kits and mission cards.
